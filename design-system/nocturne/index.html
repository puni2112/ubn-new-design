№ 04 Visual System · Nocturne

Quiet in the dark.
Potent by nature.

A dark-first identity for UBalance Naturals — a near-black forest with one luminous leaf-green glowing through it. Where the daylight systems show the plant, Nocturne shows what it does after the lights go down: restore, steadily, while you rest.

01 The Brand

Restoration happens in the dark.

UBalance Naturals makes high-potency liquid Ayurvedic formulations. Nocturne reframes the brand around recovery — the slow, unseen work the body does at rest. The design lives at night: deep, calm, with a single point of living light to mark what matters.

P.01

Calm

Darkness as composure, not drama. Generous space, low contrast everywhere except the one thing worth seeing.

P.02

Luminous

A single leaf-green glows. Light is the scarcest resource in the system — spent only on what is true and active.

P.03

Potent

Quiet on the surface, strong underneath. The depth of the ground signals concentration, not emptiness.

02 Logo

The mark stays. It learns to glow.

We keep the existing medallion wordmark — reversed to luminous white on the near-black ground, optionally lit by a faint leaf-glow halo. No recolor of the mark itself. The light comes from behind it, never from a gradient inside it.

Primary — white on near-black invert(1)
Lit — faint leaf-glow behind halo

Clear space & minimum size. Maintain padding equal to the medallion height (x) on every side. Keep the mark clear of the glow's brightest centre so it stays legible. Never set the wordmark below 26px tall.

+

Reverse to white. On any dark ground the mark is luminous white; let the background carry the colour.

Don't fill the mark with a glow or gradient, and don't add an outer stroke or neon outline.

–

Don't place on mid-tone grounds where neither white nor dark reverses cleanly.

04 Typography

Thin strokes catch the light.

Hatton — a premium high-contrast serif — leads, set light and large so its hairline strokes glow against the dark. Its italic carries every accent word, haloed in leaf-green. General Sans, a quiet grotesque, handles body, labels and UI. No monospace, no third voice.

08 Photography

Low key. One light on the glass.

Product shot in shadow — a single soft light raking the bottle, deep falloff into near-black, the botanical half-seen. Rich, saturated greens against darkness; never flat or grey. The frame fades to the page ground at its lower edge. Text never sits on the image.

Treatment

Low-key, single soft source. Deep shadow falloff into near-black; rich saturated green where the light lands.

Crop

Clean rectangle, 12px radius, edges fading into the page ground. No hard frame, no drop shadow.

Composition

The glass catches the only light; botanicals recede into shadow. Darkness is most of the frame.

09 Voice & Tone

Lower the volume. Raise the meaning.

Speak quietly and exactly. Short, calm lines; one true thing at a time. The dark system extends to language — no hype, no exclamation, no urgency. Confidence reads as stillness, and the rare bright word does the work.

We write

"While you rest, it works. 3,825 mg of actives, steadily restoring."

Calm and certain. Short fragments, one claim per line, the bright fact placed where it counts. Honest about what it does.

We avoid

"The ultimate game-changing miracle boost!!!"

No hype, no exclamation, no urgency, no emoji. Loudness is the opposite of this system; if a line shouts, rewrite it quieter.
