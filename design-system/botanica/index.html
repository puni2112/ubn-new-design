01 The Brand

We don't stimulate the body. We strengthen it.

UBalance Naturals makes high-potency liquid Ayurvedic formulations from real botanicals. Botanica tells that story in one color. A single green, deepened and lightened across a full tonal ladder — restrained, editorial and quietly premium, the way an apothecary earns trust.

P.01

Monochrome

One hue, every depth. The whole system is built from a single green ramp — contrast and hierarchy come from tone, never a competing color.

P.02

Editorial

A premium serif leads. Headlines read like a fine specimen page — generous, lettered, considered. The italic carries every accent.

P.03

Honest

Specific figures, real sources, nothing embellished. A calmer light and a single color make the facts the loudest thing on the page.

02 Logo

The mark stays. The light cools.

We keep the existing medallion wordmark in solid ink — no recolor, no gradient. Botanica only changes the field behind it: forest ink on a cool sage canvas, reversed to white on deep botanical green. The mark never takes a second color.

03 Color

One hue. Ten depths.

There is exactly one color in Botanica, expressed as a ten-step ladder from deepest pine to palest celadon. Every surface, every line, every state is drawn from this ramp. The neutrals are green-tinted; the canvas is a cool sage, not warm cream. No copper, no gold — the depth does all the work.

05 Buttons & CTA

08 Photography

Lush, living, natural light.

Product among real botanicals — root, leaf and stone — in bright, even natural light, with room to breathe. Imagery stays in full living color against the cool sage system. Frames round to 18px with a soft base shadow; never desaturated, text never on the image.

Treatment

Full natural color — rich greens, warm earth tones in the subject. Bright, even daylight; never moody.

Crop

Rounded 18px frame with a soft base shadow. No heavy vignette, no filter, no tint.

Composition

The bottle with real botanicals — roots, leaves, stone — and generous negative space.

09 Voice & Tone

Fewer words. More certainty.

Voice is unchanged across the family — confident, calm, evidence-led. Speak in short, declarative lines. State the fact, cite the number, stop. The quieter monochrome look never licenses new hype: no exclamation, no superlatives, no emoji. Confidence reads as economy.

We write

"Not a sudden rush. A steady rise in performance."

Short fragments. One claim per line. Specific numbers (3,825 mg · 247 reviews) carry the weight. Honest about what it does and doesn't do.

We avoid

"The ultimate game-changing miracle boost!!!"

No hype, no exclamation pile-ups, no vague superlatives, no emoji. If a word can be cut without losing meaning, cut it.
