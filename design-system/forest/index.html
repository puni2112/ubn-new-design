01 The Brand

We don't stimulate the body. We strengthen it.

UBalance Naturals makes high-potency liquid Ayurvedic formulations from real botanicals. The forest system speaks in living color — deep green, leaf and bark — grounded, honest and unmistakably natural.

P.01

Rooted

Grown from real botanicals, not lab shortcuts. The system stays close to the earth — natural paper, deep green, deliberate and unhurried.

P.02

P.03

Honest

02 Logo

The mark stays. The habitat changes.

We keep the existing medallion wordmark — here it lives in forest ink. No gold, no recolor. Deep green on natural paper, reversed on forest, surrounded by generous clear space.

08 Photography

Lush, living, natural light.

Product among real botanicals — root, leaf and stone — in warm natural light, with room to breathe. Imagery stays in full, living color: green and earthy, never desaturated. Soft-edged crops; text never sits on the image.

Treatment

Full natural color — rich greens, warm earth. Bright, even daylight; never moody.

Crop

Clean rectangle with a soft 8px radius. No vignette, no heavy drop shadow.

Composition

The bottle with real botanicals — roots, leaves, stone — and generous negative space.

09 Voice & Tone

Fewer words. More certainty.

Speak in short, declarative lines. State the fact, cite the number, stop. The natural system extends to language — no hype, no exclamation, no filler. Confidence reads as economy.

We write

"Not a sudden rush. A steady rise in performance."

Short fragments. One claim per line. Specific numbers (3,825 mg · 247 reviews) carry the weight. Honest about what it does and doesn't do.

We avoid

"The ultimate game-changing miracle boost!!!"

No hype, no exclamation pile-ups, no vague superlatives, no emoji. If a word can be cut without losing meaning, cut it.
