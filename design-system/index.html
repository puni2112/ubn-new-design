01 The Brand

We don't stimulate the body. We strengthen it.

UBalance Naturals makes high-potency liquid Ayurvedic formulations for holistic performance. We treat natural formulation as a system reset — addressing why modern life depletes you, not just the surface symptom.

Natural & whole

Wild-sourced Ayurvedic ingredients, nothing artificial. The brand looks and feels grown, not manufactured.

Evidence-led

Specific numbers, lab-testing, references. Trust is the design currency — every claim is backed.

Steady, not hyped

"A steady rise in performance." Calm confidence over loud urgency — composure is the premium signal.

02 Logo

An ornate medallion, a serif wordmark.

The mark pairs a compass-lotus "U" medallion with a two-line serif wordmark. Use the green-900 version on light grounds; reverse to white on dark.

Primary — on white & light surfaces · #FFFFFF / #F7F4EE
Reversed — on green-900 · filter: brightness(0) invert(1)
✓

Clear space. Keep space equal to the medallion's height on all sides. Minimum wordmark height 28px.

✓

Lock proportions. Always scale the mark uniformly; medallion and wordmark stay together.

✕

Don't recolor the mark, add shadows, outlines, or gradients, or rotate it.

✕

Don't place on low-contrast photography without a solid or scrim behind it.

04 Typography & Fonts

One geometric sans, used with intent.

Two families do all the work. Poppins carries everything visible — display, headlines, product names, body, UI and numerals; JetBrains Mono is reserved for small technical labels.

Poppins
Display · Headlines · Product names — weight 500–600
Aa Bb Cc Dd · Black Thunder Active+

Set display at weight 500–600 with tight tracking (−0.025em). Accent words inside headlines are set in copper, non-italic.

Poppins
Body · UI · Numerals — weights 400–800
Regular Medium Semibold Bold Extrabold 3,825

Body copy, labels, and all numerals/stat figures (in 800). Eyebrow numbers use Poppins 800.

07 Iconography

2px stroke. No fill. Rounded joins.

Icons follow the Lucide / Feather family — 2px stroke, no fill, round caps. They inherit currentColor. On dark grounds, set them inside a gold circle. The only non-SVG marks are ★ ✓ → ✦. No emoji, ever.

08 Photography

Bright, warm, botanical.

Amber bottle on stone, labelled botanicals, clean seamless light. Lifestyle shots are equally bright — never moody. Always framed in rounded corners; text never sits directly on the image.

09 Voice & Tone

Confident. Calm. Evidence-led.

Speak in second person, in short declarative fragments. Quantify with specific numbers. Set expectations honestly. Never hype.

We write

"Not a sudden rush. A steady rise in performance."

Short fragments for cadence. Cause → effect with arrows. Specific numbers (3,825 mg · 247 reviews) as proof. Honest disclaimers.

We avoid

"The ultimate game-changing miracle boost!!!"

No hype, no exclamation pile-ups, no vague superlatives, no emoji. Glyphs limited to ★ ✓ → ✦.
