BRAND DESIGN SYSTEMS
9 routes · ubn-new-design.vercel.app

Nine routes to the same brand. One spine, nine registers.

Every system below shares UBN's foundation — a deep forest-green spine, warm paper neutrals, the medallion mark, the same CRO structure. What changes is the typographic voice and how the green is dressed. This page is the management hand-off: read the paragraph, click through to the live system.

Shared spine · Forest green · Warm paper · Same components
00
Meridian · modern geometric
/design-system →

A clean, contemporary system built on the geometric warmth of Poppins, with JetBrains Mono for dosage and technical tags. Forest green on warm off-white, generous rounded corners and soft sage tints make it friendly, legible and current — modern wellness that reads clearly to everyone.

you want a versatile, mainstream-modern voice with the broadest reach.
01
Forest · warm forest apothecary
/design-system/forest →

"Real nature, stated plainly." Forest green on warm recycled paper with a single living-leaf accent, set in the restrained Hanken Grotesk. Softened organic corners and hairline rules give it an honest, grounded, sustainable feel.

natural credibility and calm authority matter more than decoration.
02
Apothecary · heritage letterpress
/design-system/apothecary →

A 19th-century herbal-dispensary identity: bottle-green ink on aged paper, double-rule labels, wax seals and gold-foil accents, led by the calligraphic Gambetta serif. Trades modernity for provenance and craft heritage.

you want to signal tradition, expertise and time-tested formulation.
03
Botanica · cool modern serif
/design-system/botanica →

The purist. A single green hue from deepest pine to pale celadon — no copper, no gold, no second colour anywhere — with emphasis carried entirely by the elegant Sentient serif italic. Cool, editorial and disciplined.

restraint is the luxury — a refined, monochrome statement.
04
Atelier · couture didone
/design-system/atelier →

The most premium, fashion-forward register. High-contrast Zodiak didone headlines over jewel-toned emerald gradients built entirely within the green family, paired with the refined Satoshi grotesque. Reads like a luxury beauty house.

you want to push upmarket and signal aspiration and exclusivity.
05
Aurora · dawn-to-dusk gradients
/design-system/aurora →

A dual-mode, gradient-led system with two registers: a luminous light "Dawn" and a deep dark "Dusk." Soft green-on-green tonal washes with gentle copper-to-gold glows, set in the warm Bricolage Grotesque. Dimensional and energetic without being loud.

you want warmth, depth and a contemporary digital-native feel.
06
Lab · clinical / technical
/design-system/lab →

Green reframed from nature to precision. A cool instrument-grey ground, pure-white cards, near-square corners and a grid of hairlines and registration marks — set in Swiss Supreme with a true monospace for doses, mg and assay figures. Numbers are first-class.

you want to lead with science, efficacy and proof.
07
Nocturne · low-key dark
/design-system/nocturne →

The whole identity inverted: a near-black forest canvas with a single luminous leaf-green that glows like bioluminescence. Thin, light-set Hatton serif headlines emit a soft halo against the dark. Dramatic, premium and potent-at-rest.

you want a striking, high-impact dark mode for hero moments.
08
Tactile · riso print
/design-system/tactile →

The warmly imperfect one. Two flat risograph inks — forest green and marigold — overprinting on warm newsprint with grain, halftone fills and honest misregistration, led by the weathered Erode serif. Where every sibling is precise, Tactile is hand-made.

you want craft, personality and an indie, human touch.
