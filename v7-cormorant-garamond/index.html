How quickly will I see results?
Most users notice subtle shifts (cleaner energy, less sluggishness) within the first 7 days. Stamina & reduced fatigue typically stack by week 2–4. Strength & consistency improvements emerge between Day 30–60. The full 90-day course is recommended because it works as a system reset rather than a quick fix.
Is this safe to take with caffeine or pre-workout?
Yes—Black Thunder Active+ contains zero caffeine, so it doesn't conflict with other stimulants. However, the goal is to graduate away from synthetic stimulants over time, since the formula provides sustained energy on its own.
Are there any side effects?
The formula is 100% natural with no chemicals, sugars, or synthetic additives. As with any herbal product, a small number of users report mild digestive adjustment in the first 2–3 days, which typically resolves. Not recommended for pregnant women or children. Consult your physician if you are on prescription medication.
What does it taste like?
Black Thunder Active+ has an earthy, herbal taste typical of concentrated Ayurvedic kwath formulations. Most users find it acceptable when mixed in 1/3 glass of water. The taste is the trade-off for keeping the formula 100% sugar-free and chemical-free.
Can I take this with food?
Yes. For daily use, take 15–20 ml twice daily with water—either with food or on an empty stomach, whichever works for your routine. For pre-workout use, take 30 minutes before activity for best results.
